[Pebble](http://www.pebble.by/) is a powerful tool to create shoppable and interactive videos without impacting site speed. Easily embed in stories and carousel formats with a single line of code.

* Perfect for Product Demos, UGC, Video Reviews, Brand Introductions, and more.

== Installation ==

**Requires WooCommerce 2.3.3 or higher**

Get started with our easy integration process:

**Note: If you have already completed your account setup on pebble.by dashboard, you may skip step 1.**

1. Start Your Free Trial: [Sign up here](https://join.pebble.by/?utm_source=woocommerce-plugin&utm_medium=plugin&utm_campaign=woocommerce-integration-signup).
2. Integrate with WooCommerce: In your dashboard, go to "Addons" > "WooCommerce".
3. Enter Details: Copy your App ID and Secret, and paste it in the Woocommerce plugin integration page.

That's it! Your store is now connected. A purchase is required to confirm integration success.

**To embed your pebble.by stories, carousel etc on your woocommerce product page need to edit the woocommerce template files. You can use [Edit WooCommerce Templates](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woo-edit-templates/) plugin to edit your templates, and copy/paste the respective widget codes**
